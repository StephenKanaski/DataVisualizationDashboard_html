Latitude vs. Wind Speed

The plots of wind speed (mph) vs latitudinal position suggests an incredibly weak to nonexistent correlation between the two. Wind speed, much like cloud cover and humidity, is affected by many other factors and not determined by latitude. Topography, proximity to large bodies of water, and other determinants may have an affect on the phenomena of cloud cover, humidity, and wind speed.
